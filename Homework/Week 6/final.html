Dataprocessing Week 6/7 - Mick Tozer
Source: numbeo.nl
Data: Quality of life rating for countries in Europe
SubData: Statistics that influence the QoL rating
Story: On the left you can see a ranking of EU countries based on their Quality of Life.
If you click on any of the bars representing a country on the left, the donut-chart on the right changes.
This donut-chart represents the different values/ratings for the chosen country, that influence the Quality of Life rating.
Extra: Note that, the lower the Quality of Life rating, the bigger the green section is in the donut-chart.
